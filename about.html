About

Program manager, designer and technologist, with a love for the arts. Bringing solutions to those in need, with tech as my tool. A son and the piano, bring peace to my soul. Solitude, sunsets, and mountains, a simple life I hold. Reading and learning, a journey with no end. A life full of love, laughter, and creativity, that knows no bend.

View My Works

I thrive on solving complex problems and bringing innovative ideas to life. My experience in program management has equipped me with a diverse skill set and a hands-on approach that ensures I can make a real impact in every project I tackle.

My career has been defined by adaptability and results. Whether working in fast-paced environments or managing high-pressure projects, I excel at delivering quality solutions that align with both strategic goals and emerging technologies. Staying ahead of the curve is what keeps me motivated.

Beyond my professional work, I am passionate about supporting my community, particularly through Latin Business Resource Groups. I aim to use my skills not just for career growth but to help create opportunities and make a positive difference where it matters most.
